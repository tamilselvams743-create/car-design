STANGER THINGS

Stranger Things is a sci-fi horror series set in 1980s Indiana, blending supernatural mystery, government conspiracies, and 80s nostalgia, where children and teens in the small town of Hawkins uncover a parallel dimension called the Upside Down, a dark mirror world, after a boy vanishes and a psychokinetic girl, Eleven, appears, battling monsters like the Demogorgon and the Mind Flayer that emerge from the rift.
